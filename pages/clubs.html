AMAZING KIDS KENYA

Welcome to Amazing Kids Kenya, a place where children discover their talents in a free, fun environment with positive values and excel. The club focuses on character formation through inculcation of positive values and training through creativity activities.

We take the kids through bible readings and weekly trainings that develop character, confidence, and proper skills necessary for life.

Amazing Kids Kenya takes place every Saturday at Savelberg Retreat Centre from 8:30 am to 1:00 pm for all kids aged 5-12 years.

Amazing Kids Kenya Objectives

1

To build the kids character God's way through inculcation of positive values.

2

To equip each kid with life and social skills that enhance their confidence and self-esteem.

3

To help the kids create purposeful relationships.

4

To nurture their talents through different forms of creativity in line with our theme of being amazing, original and limitless.

5

To create a fun experience where each participant feels; important, significant, special, needed, recognized, appreciated and valued.

Benefits of Art & Craft and Dancing for Kids

    Art & Craft

  • Develop fine Motor skills

    Most arts and craft projects involve moving the hands and fingers which improves the overall muscle strength and control over movements.

  • Improves Hand-Eye Coordination

    The more the children practice, the better their hand-eye coordination. This will help them in almost all walks of life.

  • Promotes innovation and creativity:

    Arts and crafts provide a platform for the kids to create new things.

  • Helps in Socializing

    Interacting with other children with the same interests gives the kids the opportunity to socialize and build friendships.

    Dancing

  • Gain Greater Self-Confidence

    The freedom to express themselves through movement and show casing themselves before others helps the kids gain.

  • Build Social Strength

    Learning how to dance is that it allows your child to become more comfortable socially.

  • Increase Fitness & Activity

    This makes a fun way to get your child fit without them even knowing it. It keeps them active and engaged, and ensures that they are going to be getting fit and into the finest shape possible.

  • Build Stronger Teamwork

    It requires an understanding of your partner's movements. With the help of this teamwork, your child will be far more likely to understand the importance of working as a team together.

  • Improve Listening & Discipline

    Your child will get a huge amount out of taking on dance lessons purely from the discipline point of view, as well. This makes them more inclined to listen to instructions, to not always expect their own way.

C5 Spark Teens Connect

Courtesy of Center of Creativity Services, the C5 Spark Teens Connect is a Christian value based club for teens between 13-17 years with weekend and holiday programs that target to engrain positive lifelong skills in Teens through creative and engaging activities.

Our teenagers face external and internal struggles between the ages of 13 to 19 years. These youngsters are expected to cope with different types of challenges and to mention but a few; hormonal changes, puberty, social, parental, academic pressures, peer pressures, and much more.

Many teens may feel misunderstood and it is vital that their feelings and thoughts are validated. Failure to do this may lead to even bigger challenges befalling the teens like: depression, stress, anxiety suicidal thoughts, unhealthy competition, bullying, drinking, smoking, underage sex, teenage pregnancy, early parenting, defiant behavior, social media addiction and the list goes on.

Against this background, Center of Creativity Services felt there was a need to start C5 Spark Connect where teenagers can meet together in an atmosphere where they can freely self-express.

C5 Spark Teens Connect is a place where they have an opportunity to feel at home, discover their self- identity, grow their faith, learn self-empowerment skills, explore their leadership skills, discover talent/become creative, build relationships, use their hands and mind in a fun, exciting and challenging way.

The different issues facing them are tackled under the umbrella of 5C's: Confidence, Curiosity, Courage, Creativity and Consistency. From these C's topical issues that relate to key challenges facing the teens will be addressed in form of diverse modules and methodology.

The C5 Spark teens connect is the place for any teen to be.

Holiday Programs

Every school Holiday, Center Creativity Services organizes powerful exciting programs for both kids (under Amazing Kids Kenya) and for teens (under C5 teens connect club).

Each school holiday is given a different approach:

Survival Kit Series (April)

In this, participants are equipped with skills to help them confront with ease various challenges and emergencies that occur in our day to day lives. They practice applying these skills in real-life scenarios, and case studies.

Treasure Hunt Series (August)

Each person is created with God given potential which involves aligning our desires and actions with God's will for our lives. Everybody has a God given desire to live to full potential but unfortunately, many people do not reach it as majority of the people are caught up in the everyday which becomes easier to continue, hence maintaining the status quo or succumbing to peer pressure.

This results in people failing to lead authentic lives copying what is trending or popular in order to fit in. They give in to peer pressure especially with the influence of the social media.

In the series, we cover different topics to help the participants to dig their very core to get the treasure that lies inside each one of them.

Charting a unique path for my life series (November/December)

In a world where moral values have really become rare, and cut throat competition is in all aspects of life, we at Center of Creativity Services, believe that there is an urgent need for preparing our young to become unique and different, so that they can stand out and become the light increasing their chances of being picked out in a crowd for different positions.

Under the unique path series, we develop programs that will help the children attain a competitive edge in whichever situation they may find themselves in.

NOTE: These holiday program are mainly organized as sleeping camps which take 2-4 nights/days

Creative Campers and workshops

These are day programs which are organized covering different activities. Mainly, they take place in November and December.

Expected outcomes of these programs

  • Enhanced self-esteem leading to high confidence levels
  • Living a purpose driven life with impact.
