CREATING A BUSINESS LOGO

A business logo is the face of your business. It identifies your business. When people think about your business, the first thing they see is your logo. Just the same as when you try to remember someone, the first thing you remember about them is their face.

Knowing this, as a business kid who's been with us through the Amazing Kids' entrepreneurship classes, would you rather want your business remembered as an attractive business or just meh!? I bet you would like your business to be as beautiful and attractive as possible wouldn't you?

You can design your own business logo from scratch: from the design to the colors, or you could hire a professional to follow your ideas and create a good business logo for you. Either path works fine. However, if you chose the hiring route, make sure to give your ideas to the professional on what you want in your logo so that it can be something that you feel a connection to. Because after all the business is yours and you'd better love the way it looks otherwise you are bound to lose motivation in making it successful.

If you're the brave ones who chose to create their own logo from start, make sure at the end to transfer your final sketch to a professional design platform for a professional look. You could ask your guardian or older siblings for help in this if you need to. Great logo design tools like logo maker or canva will do the job perfectly! PS that's what we as Amazing Kids used for this class and we are so proud of the end result.

Before showcasing our business logos, it is of huge importance for you to know why having a good logo is good for your business. The most important aspect is that it grabs attention and attracts customers to your shop. Ultimately it's a potential customer's first impression of your business and separates you from the crowd. Here's one I bet you didn't know about, your customers expect it. The first thing customers always look for in your business is your logo. Therefore, this should be a motivation for you to create not just a logo, but a very attractive logo. It really goes the mile in making your business successful.
