Money Masterly Mindset

Become a Money Master by developing a Money Masterly Mindset

Theme

Success is when preparedness meets opportunity

Key focus

Money and core values

Primary Objective

To empower children and teens with practical financial literacy skills rooted in biblical principles.

In the program, the participants will be equipped to understand money, hard work, saving, giving, responsibility and more as part of their God-given purpose.

Execution Methodology

  • Lessons and devotion time.
  • Practical's through creativity and team building activities.
  • Case studies and group work.

Extra Activities

  • Sport Competitions: Outdoor games and challenges.
  • Adventure: Biking, team obstacle moments.
  • Team Building: Collaboration challenges and curiosity hunts.
  • Creative Zone: Art, skits, and music to show what they've learned about money and values.
  • Public Speaking Sessions: speaking and presentation training.

Dates (November 2025)

Week one: 10th to 14th: Amazing Kids aged 5-12 years.

Week Two: 17th to 21st: The C5 Spark Teens Connect for teens aged: 13 - 17 years.

Venue: Children's Car Park, Nyerere Road, Nairobi

Time: 8:30 am to 4.00 pm daily.

Cost: Kshs 11,000/- PP, which is inclusive of facilitation and meals.

Discounts: Applicable for more than 2 participants and early birds payments.
