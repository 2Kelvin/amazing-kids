    • #1 CENTER OF CHOICE

    Center of Creativity Services is a Christian and positive values based organization focusing on solutions for personal development needs of people as they go through their life cycle. We create opportunities that enrich, empower and transform their lives by engaging, supporting and promoting need-based training, mentoring, coaching, creativity and other different forms of personal management and empowerment to nurture their development and growth.

    Under the Center, we have Amazing Kids which deals with the children, C5 Spark Teens Connect which deals with the teens and Amazing Personalities which deals with the youth, adults and seniors' programs.

    Our vision is to be the number one choice of change in empowering, equipping and enriching lives.

    Our mission is to identify unmet needs of people and design enriching, empowering and transformational programs that add value and make a remarkable impact in their lives.

    Our values are:

    • Integrity
    • Dependable & Reliable
    • Innovation
    • Passion
    • Diversity
    • Courage
    • Proactive & Consistent

    Our primary objective is to live up to our mission of structuring programs that are suitable for each age group and to have a unique and superb center, giving people opportunities to be creative and leverage on the programs and activities offered for life time impact.
